Our Story

Overview

High Precision Car Wash Hygiene and Cleaning Specialist (Pty) Ltd is a 100% black-owned company formed and registered with CIPC in 2017. Our aim is offering a wide range of high-quality products and services to our clients.

We were established to provide professional work and quality services, focusing on fulfilling our clients' needs without compromising our integrity and high standards.
